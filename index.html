Top Three Most Loved Dog Breeds


whether you own one of theses breeds or just love animals, here is the top three dog breeds in the USA


1. Labradors

These lovable and silly dogs are a treat for any family. Loyal, Friendly, and Truly mans best friend.
They know how to work hard and play harder.

2. French Bulldogs

French Bulldogs are small breed but tough for their size. They are great with people, kids, and other dogs,
but watch out for their big personalities and stuborn temperament.

3. Golden Retrievers

These beautiful animals have elegant coats and a great personality. Being know for its goofy temperament
and strong desire to be in a family the Golden Retreiever is definitely a great family dog

Summary

With many dog breeds in the world it really is hard to choose the top 3 dogs.
After doing months of researth we were able to narrow it down to these three dog
and its plain to see why they made the top of the list. follow along at the bottom
